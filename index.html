Hello World!

My name is Nick Linck and I am currently a cognitive developer working for IBM research in San Jose, California. I recently graduated from the University of Michigan in 2018 with a double major in Computer Science Engineering and Data Science Engineering and a minor in Physics. In my life, I want to be continuously learning and applying what I have learned to help others. My hobbies involve living a healthy life by cooking with whole foods, exercising (playing sports, dancing, yoga, running), and meditation. I love being in nature to hike, camp, swim, and photograph.

In my professional life, I hope to create technology that will allow individuals to make a living doing what they love. This desire arose from my interest in machine learning, which is fueled by my desire to combine my technical knowledge with my unending interest in how the human brain works. The field of machine learning fascinates me because of its wide range of uses, from saving lives with better patient care, to finally answering the question 'hot dogs or legs?' The applications appear endless making me excited to see how I can apply machine learning to help people live even happier lives, but the endless applications make me fear that many jobs are at risk! This is why I hope to create technology that will allow individuals to easily share with the world their talents (teaching yoga, music, painting, cooking, excersising...), in hope that others will find value in learning from somoneone who loves what they do. Feel free to reach out to me for any reason!
