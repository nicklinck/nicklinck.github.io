Hello World!

My name is Nick Linck and I am currently a Research Scientist at IBM in San Jose, California. My reason for pursuing computer science has been motivated by my love for creating and by my unending interest in the human brain. As of Jan 2021, I am working on implementing psychological theories for how the human brain works, into AI, with the goal of creating AI that can generalize to new situations like humans (see our paper which was accepted to AAAI 2021 for an overview). I am also researching how to most effectively stop the spread of fake news, focused now on making it simple for a user to come to their own conclusion that what they saw/read was false. Finally, I am researching how to use machine learning to protect from cyber attacks, such as ransomware, in a cloud native environment.


Outside of my research life, I love learning to play instruments and to produce music, exercising (tennis, dancing, slacklining, yoga, volleyball, swimming), cooking, and spending time with people.


Future aspirations: many! My research interests are broad and generally focused on using technology to improve life. Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect!
