Hello World!

My name is Nick Linck and I am currently a senior at the University of Michigan pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering and Data Science Engineering with a minor in Physics. My passions lie in combining my technical knowledge with my unending interest in how the human brain works. This leads me to fields related to data analysis, user experience, machine learning, and human/computer interactions. These fields really excite me because I love trying to understand what goes on in our brains. I am constantly questioning why others, and myself, act the way we do hoping to uncover exactly how our brain works. Thus, working a project where it is crucial to think about how someone will react to a product really appeals to me. Additionally, I love working with data. Data can provide statistical evidence for analyzing these human interactions, and data can allow machines to make inferences about the data that a human may have never seen. The field of machine learning really excites me because of its wide range of uses. From saving lives with better patient care, to finally answering the question 'hot dogs or legs?', the applications appear endless. Moreover, the thought of creating artificial general intelligence excites me most of all. It has the possibility to find cures to every disease, understand how our world works, and uncover answers to questions that we have not even thought of asking. If you have an opportunity up any of these alleys, reach out to me!
